About Me

Get to know the developer behind the code

My name is Evan Gardner. I was born in Syracuse, New York on July 16, 2009. I live with my mom, dad, brother, two dogs and three cats, and I love them all, especially my cats. I have some experience working with computers from Seaway Tech’s Computer & Information Science class, specifically with cybersecurity, and creating websites.
